5 Cheesey Jokes for Any Social Situation

You know what they say. Laughter is the best medicine! What better to make you laugh than everyday "dad jokes." Here are 5 funny, cheesey jokes that made me chuckle today!

1. "Why don't eggs tell jokes?"

They'd crack each other up.

2. "Do you know why I don't trust stairs?"

They're always up to something.

3. "Why couldn't the bicycle stand up by itself?"

It was two tired.

4. "Why did the math book look so sad?"

Because of all of its problems.

5. "What kind of shoes do ninjas wear?"

Sneakers.

I hope you enjoyed these jokes as much as I did!
